Our Mission:

Downie. Inc. is dedicated to empowering individuals with Down Syndrome and their families through awareness, education, and community support. We strive to create a world where people with Down Syndrome are recognized for their unique abilities and potential, promoting inclusion, equality, and respect in all aspects of society. Our mission is to serve as a beacon of hope and a source of valuable resources, advocating for the rights and opportunities of every individual with Down Syndrome to lead fulfilling lives. Together, we aim to inspire positive change, celebrate differences, and build a more inclusive community for all.

Our Team: The Heart of Downie. Inc.

At Downie. Inc., our strength lies in the diversity and dedication of our team. Each member brings unique skills, experiences, and perspectives that enrich our mission to support and empower the Down Syndrome community.

Maria Thompson, Community Outreach Coordinator

Maria's passion for inclusivity shines through her work in creating engaging community programs and events. Diagnosed with Down Syndrome at birth, Maria has never let that define her limits. Instead, she uses her experiences to connect deeply with individuals and families, spreading awareness and fostering a sense of belonging. Her creativity and warmth make her an indispensable part of our outreach efforts.

Elijah Kim, Creative Director

Elijah's artistic vision has been instrumental in shaping our brand's voice and image. With a keen eye for design and a deep understanding of the power of visual storytelling, he crafts compelling campaigns that capture the essence of our cause. Elijah's commitment to representing the diverse experiences of those with Down Syndrome has elevated our message, touching hearts and minds across the globe.

Sophie Patel, Educational Program Manager

Sophie leads our educational initiatives with a focus on developing resources and workshops that enhance learning and development opportunities for individuals with Down Syndrome. Her background in special education and her innovative approach to curriculum design have resulted in programs that not only educate but also inspire confidence and independence.

Alex Johnson, Volunteer Coordinator

Alex's organizational skills and empathetic leadership ensure our volunteer programs run smoothly and effectively. He excels at matching volunteers' skills with the needs of our community, creating rewarding experiences for everyone involved. Alex, whose younger sister has Down Syndrome, is deeply motivated by the joy and growth he sees in both volunteers and participants

Tara Singh, Advocacy and Policy Specialist

Tara is the force behind our advocacy efforts, tirelessly working to promote policies that support the rights and opportunities of people with Down Syndrome. Her legal expertise and persuasive communication skills have been pivotal in advancing our cause at both the local and national levels.
